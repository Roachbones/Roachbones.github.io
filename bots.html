bots

These are social media bots that post things automatically.

  • @validpolls tweets yes/no polls, asking questions in the form of "Is it [valid/problematic/etc] to [transitive verb] [noun]?"
  • @tifutldr tweets TL;DRs from r/tifu. it's NSFW.
  • @userboxbot tweets userbox text from Wikipedia.
  • @shinpachibot tweets quotes from Shimura Shinpachi, from Gintama.
  • userbox-ebooks generates userboxes with markov chains. it's on tumblr.
